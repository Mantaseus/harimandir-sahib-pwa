English Translation :
BILAAVAL, FIFTH MEHL:

One who forgets God is filthy, poor and low. The fool does not understand the Creator Lord; instead, he thinks that he himself is the doer. || 1 || Pain comes, when one forgets Him. Peace comes when one remembers God. This is the way the Saints are in bliss — they continually sing the Glorious Praises of the Lord. || 1 || Pause || The high, He makes low, and the low, he elevates in an instant. The value of the glory of our Lord and Master cannot be estimated. || 2 || While he gazes upon beautiful dramas and plays, the day of his departure dawns. The dream becomes the dream, and his actions do not go along with him. || 3 || God is All-powerful, the Cause of causes; I seek Your Sanctuary. Day and night, Nanak meditates on the Lord; forever and ever he is a sacrifice. || 4 || 20 || 50 ||

Thursday, 25th Phalgun (Samvat 554 Nanakshahi) (Page: 813)
