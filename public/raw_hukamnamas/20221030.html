English Translation :
AASAA:

Why bother to read the Simritees to a dog? Why bother to sing the Lord’s Praises to the faithless cynic? || 1 || Remain absorbed in the Lord’s Name, Raam, Raam, Raam. Do not bother to speak of it to the faithless cynic, even by mistake. || 1 || Pause || Why offer camphor to a crow? Why give the snake milk to drink? || 2 || Joining the Sat Sangat, the True Congregation, discriminating understanding is attained. That iron which touches the Philosopher’s Stone becomes gold. || 3 || The dog, the faithless cynic, does everything as the Lord causes him to do. He does the deeds pre-ordained from the very beginning. || 4 || If you take Ambrosial Nectar and irrigate the neem tree with it, still, its natural qualities are not changed. || 5 || 7 || 20 ||
